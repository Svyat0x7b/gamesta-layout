Gamesta dApp

is a BigData, AI powered resource across the entire spectrum of the GameFi and P2E landscape, providing data driven and informed decisions.

M.L. & A.I Powered

GAMESTA is developing an M.L. and Neural Network based algorithm to predict values of in-game assets, assisting players and investors to maximise time and earnings. A.I. algorithms will optimise P2E rewards by matching players and assets to their skill levels while promoting G-Guild scholars based on results.

G-Guild

Connecting NFT holders directly to the players, enabling direct profit without a middle man. A.I. tech improves decision making ensuring maximum yield. G-Guild handles admin associated with guilds and supplies tools and data to game developers, letting them focus on the game.

Wrapped NFTs

NFT holders deposit NFTs to be utilised by players and receive a wrapped and/or mirrored version in exchange allowing the wrapped NFT to still be traded in the marketplace. NFT holders and players can track associated performance history, trends, and data based valuations.

Marketplace

GAMESTA marketplace will serve as a hub for the lending, trading, and buying of gaming NFTs from any of the supported Blockchains in one convenient location. Income generated by the GAMESTA marketplace will be handled in the same process as the GAMESTA Treasury.
